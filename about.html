Who am I?

My comfort place is my curiosity! I'm a person who questions everything, tries to understand the world and has found the answers through the STEM field. My curiosity could inspire answers that, combined with technology, could serve to create solutions to real-world problems.

I come from a city in the Southern Brazilian countryside with 30,000 inhabitants, Santa Vitória do Palmar. I know what it's like to grow up without opportunities, so now I use technology as a tool for accessibility and empowerment for communities.
