A world beyond the southern Brazilian countryside.

My passions led me to powerful experiences! I was a math enthusiast, being a TA for 4 years at my school, developing a workbook with 350+ math exercises for a basic math course and created an Instagram (Wemath) to support students after the pandemic (reached 80k+ views). Fascinated by mathematics, I decided to go beyond books and studied its applications in technology. I studied Computer Science at the Cambridge University, where I immersed myself in the world of technology and fell in love. After that, I never stopped!

Technology as a tool for inclusion.

I've been using technology to fight for causes I believe in, promoting social inclusion of deaf people, LGBTQ+ people at the job market (safely), and women.

Finding my community.

As part of the Latin American Leadership Academy, I always work with other Latinx changemakers to create a world more welcoming. Also, empowering young girls in the field of technology, as an Ambassador for Conectadas Program, make my heart overflow. These communities have shaped who I am today! I want to keep carrying on their values to make the world a place for everyone.
